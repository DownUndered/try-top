safety

operating and safe play instructions

Our product manufactured and meet mandatory and volunteer safety requirements!

To reduce the risk of injury you must follow the below important precautions and instructions. For complete safety information of the equipment please refer to the owner’s manual.

Each product comes with a detailed User's manual, containing technical specifications of the product, its assembly, use and maintanance instructions, as well as safety precautions.

warning 3+

  • Continuous adult supervision required for all ages while using the playground
  • Do not permit children to use the equipment in manner for which it was not intended
  • Owners should be responsible for maintaining the legibility of warning labels
  • At all times you must follow safe instructions and observe the capacity limitations of playground set
  • Install and maintain shock absorbing material under and around the playground
  • Do not allow children to get off or jump from trapeze or rings while they are in motion
  • No obstacles or toys should be placed under or close to the equipment
  • Place playground away from windows, furniture electrical power points
  • Check for any loose elements, replace, tighten and or sand smooth as required
  • Never allow kids to play with the ropes, pet leashes, clotheslines, cables, chains, other cord-like items when using the playground or to attach ropes from playset
  • Never allow kids to use loose fitting clothes, ponchos, hoods, scarfs, capes, necklaces, items with draw strings, cords or ties using the playground
  • Do not allow children to wear open toe or heel footwear like sandals, flip flops or clogs
  • Do not allow kids to play on the playground until the assembly is completed. Keep small parts, sharp parts and part with sharp points away from children at any time
