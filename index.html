Our Playgrounds are Advanced Indoor Sport Gyms

for the whole family.


They include Swedish wall, rope ladder, gymnastic rope, gymnastic trapeze, rings, bars and punching bags!


Could be installed at home, in apartment, in any room or yard. They are firmly attached to the wall and safely hold from 120 to 200 kg, depending on a model.


All attachment elements are adjustable by height. If you attach them on beams you can adjust on any level from the ground.



Children will enjoy the skills they develop while using this fun piece of equipment. 

It is easy to use the climbing steps to get to the top bar to do pull ups or leg lifts. The climbing action will significantly target legs, engaging the quad and calve muscles while strengthening important ligaments of the knee. Children also use the arms and increase cardiovascular endurance with each repetition.

The playground can be used for various exercising, stretching elements for legs, back and the whole body, as well as for push ups and pull ups, ABC strengthen elements.
This will be beneficial in many sports 


The steps have the unique technology of massage + anti slip cover​

It ensures a firm and stronger grip.

Micromassage effect of the steps helps to promote motor skills of children and prevention of flatfoot
